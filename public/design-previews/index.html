Design Previews

Four UI/UX directions for Baseball Ledger. Click to view full mockups.

Wrigley
Brick + Ivy + Cream

A: Wrigley Ivy

Warm cream base with brick red header and ivy green accents. Inspired by the Friendly Confines -- outfield ivy, exposed brick, and sun-baked scoreboards. Bitter slab-serif headlines.

Light Theme Bitter Brick + Ivy
Ledger
Navy + Silver + White

B: The Blue Book

Clean professional aesthetic with deep navy header, silver accents, and bright white cards. The official record keeper's guide. Fjalla One bold condensed headlines, cool gray palette.

Light Theme Fjalla One Navy + Silver
Monster
Dark / Scoreboard

C: The Green Monster

Dark scoreboard green with gold/cream text. Feels like looking at a manual Fenway scoreboard. Oswald condensed headlines, amber LED indicators, dramatic shadows.

Dark Theme Oswald Green + Gold
Pennant
Red / White / Blue

D: Pennant Race

Bold Americana with patriot blue and stitch red on cream white. Stars-and-stripes energy -- pennant banners, scoreboard LEDs, and Opening Day bunting. Teko tall condensed display type.

Light Theme Teko Red + Blue
